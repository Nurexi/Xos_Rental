Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our rental services

What documents do I need to rent a vehicle?

To rent a vehicle, you'll need a valid driver's license, a credit card in your name, and proof of insurance. For motorcycle rentals, you'll need a valid motorcycle license or endorsement. International customers will need a passport and may require an International Driving Permit.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our standard cancellation policy allows for full refunds if cancelled 48 hours before the scheduled pickup time. Premium and Luxury plans offer free cancellation up to 24 hours before pickup. Cancellations made after these timeframes may be subject to a fee.

Is insurance included in the rental price?

Basic third-party insurance is included with all rentals. Comprehensive and full coverage insurance are included with Premium and Luxury plans respectively. Additional insurance options are available for purchase at the time of booking.

Can I rent a vehicle if I'm under 25 years old?

Yes, drivers between 21-24 years old can rent most vehicles, but may be subject to a young driver surcharge. Luxury and high-performance vehicles may have a minimum age requirement of 25 or 30 years depending on the model.

Do you offer one-way rentals?

Yes, we offer one-way rentals between our locations for an additional fee. The fee varies depending on the distance between pickup and drop-off locations. Please contact us for specific pricing on your desired route.
